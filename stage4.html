KEY STAGE FOUR

Our Key Stage 4 students are fully supported to make their GCSE choices from a range of over 20 courses.



Alongside the academic programme, we offer a full Enrichment programme and students are encouraged to engage in a vast and wide range of options from sporting to the creative to the academic as well as activities that are more community-based. Designed to explore future careers as well as stretching natural talents, the secondary enrichment courses aim to inspire, challenge and engage them.

As a school we believe that:

All children are talented, unique and gifted in a special way. Let us grow with with and nurture your child in a way that will make him or her an internationally responsible citizen
